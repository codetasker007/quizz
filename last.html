Let's start with your morning routine

Step 1 - Morning Cleanser: A low-PH cleanser in your morning skincare routine helps to properly deep clean without causing redness that is hard to calm before makeup application.

Step 2 - Vitamin C Serum: Your face is up against a lot of agents that wish to do it harm throughout the day (exhaust fumes, your dirty phone screen, etc). A Vitamin C Serum sees to it that none of those stressors cause long term damage to your skin.

Step 3 - Moisturizer: There is no such thing as skin that is too hydrated and there is no replacement for this essential step.

Step 4 - Sunscreen: If you only had time for one skincare product each morning, it had better be sunscreen. SPF is non-negotiable!

Let's start with your morning routine

Step 1 - Night Cleanser: A cleanser is necessary to lift and remove free radical damaging agents at the end of the day.

Step 2 - Toner: Essential for balancing skin pH and prepping skin the ingredients that follow. After patting your skin dry with a towel, apply the product with your fingers, not with a cotton pad.

Step 3 - Serum/Treatments: This is the most powerful and customizable step in your routine, used to treat the issues you’re having.

Step 4 - Night Cream: The final step in your night routine should always be a viscous moisturizer to seal in the effects of all the hard work you’ve put in to the previous steps.

NOTE: Sleeping masks and night creams are intended to still be lingering in the AM, therefore also starting your morning routine with a cleanser is an absolute must in order to begin your day with properly cleansed skin.
